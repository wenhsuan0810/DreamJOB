🎤 Interview Preparation 面試準備

貼上目標職缺 JD:預測面試題、產生 BEAT 自我介紹、找出弱點題與可轉移能力, 再用計時練習模式實戰演練 — 中英文皆可。

🎯 針對目標職缺準備 Prepare for a specific JD

貼上 JD 並按「分析」,下方會自動產生:預測面試題(含 STAR 答題要點)、弱點題預備、以及與你 Profile CV 對照的可轉移能力。

🔮 預測面試題 Predicted questions(8–10 題)

依 JD 內容推斷的 behavioral / situational 題目,每題附 STAR 答題要點。點開逐題準備,並在計時練習模式中演練。

🛡️ 弱點題預備 Weakness prep — 可轉移能力

JD 要求但你 CV 中沒有出現的關鍵字 — 面試官很可能追問。別硬拗,用「可轉移能力」框架回答: 承認差距 → 指出最相近的實戰經驗 → 說明你如何快速補上。

🎬 自我介紹產生器 — BEAT 架構

Background(背景)→ Experience(經歷)→ Achievement(亮點成就)→ Type of person(我是什麼樣的人)。 填入四段素材,依目標公司的痛點客製你的亮點;產出需與 CV 和 Cover Letter 一致。

❓ 反問面試官 Questions to ask(選 3–5 題)

面試尾聲一定會被問「你有什麼問題想問我們?」。勾選 3–5 題帶進場,按複製直接存到手機備忘錄。

建議 3–5 題;最後一題永遠問時程 next steps。

⭐ The STAR method — your story skeleton

Every behavioral answer ("Tell me about a time when…") should follow this structure. Aim for 1.5–2 minutes per story, weighted toward Action and Result.

S

Situation (~15 sec)

Set the scene in 2 sentences. Company, project, what was at stake. "Our checkout error rate spiked to 8% the week before Double 11."

T

Task (~10 sec)

Your specific responsibility — what were you on the hook for? "As the on-call engineer, I owned finding and fixing the root cause."

A

Action (~60 sec)

The heart of the story. 3–4 concrete steps you took, decisions you made, trade-offs you weighed. Use "I", not "we".

R

Result (~20 sec)

Quantified outcome + what you learned. "Error rate fell to 0.5%, we cleared Double 11 with record sales, and I added an alert so it can't recur silently."
