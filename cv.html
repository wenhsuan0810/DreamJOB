📚 Golden rules of a strong CV

1️⃣
Start bullets with strong action verbsLed, built, launched, reduced, negotiated — never "responsible for".
2️⃣
Quantify everything you can"Cut deploy time 40%" beats "improved deployment process" every time.
3️⃣
Tailor to each jobMirror the language of the job description for both ATS and human readers.
4️⃣
Keep it tight1 page for <10 years of experience, 2 pages max. Cut anything that doesn't sell you.
5️⃣
Show impact, not dutiesWhat changed because you were there? Revenue, speed, quality, cost, users.
6️⃣
Make it skimmableRecruiters spend ~7 seconds on the first pass. Bullets, white space, consistent format.
